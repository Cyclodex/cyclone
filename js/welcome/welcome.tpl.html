Welcome to Cyclone beta4

Improvements:

  • Cyclone will now try to detect when a new day starts. It will automatically switch to the new day.
    This will only happen if you left Cyclone on the current day.
  • Once on a new day, Cyclone will ask you when you started the day and might have some suggestions. (whenever it detects)
  • Timeformat: can now be changed to other formats.
  • Copy format: The copied time when marking an entry as done is now also configurable.
Visit your profile, to set your own settings for the available features. Or just enjoy the defaults.
